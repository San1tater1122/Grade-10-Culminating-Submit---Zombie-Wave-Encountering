# When you get a talent, your talent icon will be displayed in the upper right corner 

### Fast Reload

### MultiShot
![multiShot](https://github.com/San1tater1122/G10Culminating---Zombie-Wave-Encountering/assets/98987733/71fccb3c-f229-401e-886d-dfa2e288b055)
Makes your crossbow shoot 3 arrows at a time like a shotgun instead of 1

![quiverIcon](https://github.com/San1tater1122/G10Culminating---Zombie-Wave-Encountering/assets/98987733/35798ec1-4db2-48d9-85b2-b4c93ddf8140)
